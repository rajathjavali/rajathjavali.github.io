Fifa World Cup
Visualizes data on past Fifa World Cup matches on a map and bar chart. BarChart click gives current year info on the map and side panel, with winning and runner up teams. On click of a country on the map displays all the years in which it took part in the World Cup. It also allows barchart to be visualized on different parameters such as Attendance, Teams, Matches, Goals.
Fifa World Cup Brazil
Visualizes data on Fifa World Cup Brazil as an interactive table and a tree representing the rounds. Table collapses or expands on clicking a row showing details of all the matches. Hovering over the table rows, it also highlights the teams progress on the tree. Hovering over the goals visualization displays info in a tool tip. Any column can be sorting in ascending or descending order based on 2 clicks or single click.
United States Elections
Visualizes data on United States Election from the year 1940 to 2012. Clicking a perticular year gives in depth details. It has StateWise winner data represented as bars with width representing the total Electorial Votes corresponding to that state. And color representing the percentage by which Republic or Independent or Democrat won in that state. It has another visualization which represents the popularity votes of the nominated candidates for the 3 parties. Finally the Map visualization shows the statewise data, with expanded details on hover. We also have a brush selection, which gives the trend for the selected states for the selected years.
